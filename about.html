D'Urban Fitness was established in 2012 by Jeanette and Ryan after moving to the UK from Durban, South Africa. Everyone has their own goals and we are here to help you achieve and maintain YOUR fitness goals. Expect high-vitality quick-paced exercises that leave each muscle shaking.

D'Urban Fitness offers a great selection of virtual classes run by our very own Fitness Class Instructors - so you can get to the classes you love with your favourite instructors leading the way. With a variety of classes available, including Pilates, HIIT, LIIT, Yoga, and Aerobics. we'll help you achieve your goals. Whether that's building your fitness or increasing your strength, and - most importantly giving you a boost of confidence and encouragement.

We have tons of equipment to be used of freely by any of our members, but there will alaways be a team member nearby to ensure the equipment is being made use of appropriately and safely. From treadmills and free-weights, to ropes and medicine balls - we have it all! We also have a boxing section with a ring and punching bags.

Our staff are all qualified and certified trainers. They are always on hand to help and guide you throughout your exercises, or even if you just want to have a little chat. Do not be shy to approach anyone in the gym, we are all there to help - both staff and other members.
