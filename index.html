Who we are

D'Urban Fitness is a unique fitness studio in Windsor with an incredibly supportive and friendly atmosphere. We pride ourselves on our ZERO JUDGEMENT policy! We understand how hard it can be to get yourself into a gym, especially if you've never stepped foot in one. We want you to feel comfortable and empowered when you walk through our doors!

Offering a range of fitness classes, we help all of our clients achieve their common goal - to build confidence and to look and to feel better. Classes can be participated both in person and virtually or a combination of both, whichever suits our clients schedules most. Our smaller class sizes allow us to concentrate on individuals within class meaning that at D'Urban Fitness, no client gets forgotten about or left behind.
